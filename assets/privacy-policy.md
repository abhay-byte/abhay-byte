# Privacy Policy for FactualStats

**Effective Date:** July 6, 2026

## Overview
FactualStats ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices when you use the FactualStats Android application (the "App").

FactualStats is a Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) application designed to provide a real-time, customizable performance HUD overlay for your Android device.

## Data Collection and Usage
**We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, usage data, or analytics of any sort.**

FactualStats operates entirely locally on your device. The App reads device hardware and performance metrics—such as CPU/GPU frequency and load, frametime graphs, FPS, device temperatures, memory usage, and battery statistics—solely for the purpose of displaying them to you in real-time via the HUD overlay. None of this data is logged permanently or sent to any external servers.

## In-App Purchases (IAP)
FactualStats is a free application, but we may offer optional In-App Purchases (IAPs) to allow users to support the project's ongoing development. 

All payment processing is handled securely and entirely by the respective app store provider (e.g., Google Play Store). FactualStats does not collect, process, or have access to your financial information, billing details, or personal identity related to these transactions. Please refer to the privacy policy of your app store provider for details on how they handle your payment data.

## Permissions
To function correctly, FactualStats requires certain on-device permissions:
- **Display Over Other Apps (Overlay):** Used strictly to draw the performance HUD on top of games and other apps.
- **Usage Access / Shizuku / Wireless Debugging:** Used strictly to read deep system sensors and performance metrics that are otherwise inaccessible.

The data accessed through these permissions remains on your device and is never transmitted externally.

## Open Source Transparency
Because FactualStats is fully open-source, anyone can inspect the code to verify that it functions exactly as described and that no hidden data collection occurs. 

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time if the App's feature set changes. However, our core commitment to collecting **zero user data** will not change. Any updates will be reflected by the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.

## Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out by opening an issue on the FactualStats GitHub repository.
